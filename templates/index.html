01. About Me

I'm a curious builder who fell in love with the moment a green ✅ appears in a CI pipeline. My journey started with static HTML pages; now I package apps into containers, wire up automated tests, and deploy to the cloud.

When something breaks, I don't panic — I read the logs. When something works, I ask: "how do I make this repeatable?" That question is basically the whole of DevOps.
